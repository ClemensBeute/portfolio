seashells

These Seashells where made on a vacation in Denmark. While wandering in the mud flats, my node brain just couldn't resist :) I had to recreate all those beautiful seashells like whelks, common cockles and razor clams in blender. Everything is done procedural with Geometry Nodes and Shader Nodes.

If you want these Models, you can get them on blenderkit:

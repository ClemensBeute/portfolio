extraterrestrial relic

This alien egg was created in the context of Nodevember 2022. As i saw this prompt, I wanted to explore the art style of HR Giger. I started with a quick scribble and then jumped directly into Blender. The whole Scene is completely Procedural, wich made it possible to quickly create multiple versions by just changing some values. Created with Geometry Nodes and Shader Nodes.
